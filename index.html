Seam carving

Implementation of a seam carving algorithm for content aware image resizing

Download this project as a .zip file Download this project as a tar.gz file

User interface and features

  • Inserting and removing vertical seams (this affects the width of the image)
  • Visualising the energy, the accumulated energy and seams
  • The option to use forward energy while calculating the accumulated energy

Note: This was written for a school assignment and it's far from optimal and still lacks a lot of features. Do not expect high performance or high quality.

Conclusion

Seam carving has a lot of benefits over traditional scaling and cropping. Important objects in the image maintain their properties because they aren't stretched out or clipped. Better results can be achieved by helping the algorithm by labeling the image with areas that are important and areas that aren't. This information can be used in the accumulated energy image. Overall the algorithm performs very well on his own. With the use of forward energy, lines stayed straight after resizing. There are still a lot of performance tweaks possible. For example, it's not necessary to recompute the energy and accumulated energy matrices after a seam has been deleted. Only a subset of items are affected.
